Features of Our Technology

Engineered for speed, sensitivity, and simplicity in single-molecule protein analytics

We build an easy-to-use benchtop device designed specifically for laboratory environments, enabling researchers to perform advanced protein analysis without specialized training or complex protocols.

  • High throughput: Process large sample sets efficiently.
  • 1 microliter sample requirement: Preserve precious material.
  • Add, click button, and read: Streamlined workflow from start to result.
  • 1 second per well by sequential analysis: Rapid data acquisition.
  • Range 10 pM – 10 nM: Wide dynamic range for diverse assays.
  • LoD 10 picomolars: Exceptional sensitivity for low-abundance targets.
  • Glycosylation features: Sialylation, mannosylation, galactosylation, and fucosylation in ~2 minutes per feature.
  • Most sensitive titer assay worldwide: Industry-leading precision.
  • Fastest glycoprofiling worldwide: Accelerate discovery and QC.
